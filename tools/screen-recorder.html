What Free Online Screen Recorder do?

Free Online Screen Recorder is a web-based tool that allows you to capture your screen activity along with audio input. It is ideal for creating tutorials, recording presentations, or capturing gameplay without the need for any software installation.


How to use Free Online Screen Recorder?

  1. 1. Click the "Start Recording" button to begin capturing your screen.
  2. 2. Select the screen or application window you want to record.
  3. 3. To stop recording, click the "Stop Recording" button.
  4. 4. Preview your recording in the video player provided.
  5. 5. Click the "Download Recording" button to save the video file to your device.
