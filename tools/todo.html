    How to Use the To-Do List App

    This simple to-do list app allows you to manage your tasks efficiently. You can add new tasks, mark them as completed, and filter them based on their status. Your tasks are saved automatically in your browser's local storage, so they will be available even after you close the tab.


    • Add Tasks: Type a task in the input field and click "Add" or press Enter.
    • Mark as Completed: Click the checkbox next to a task to mark it as completed.
    • Filter Tasks: Use the filter buttons to view all tasks, only active tasks, or only completed tasks.
    • Clear Completed: Click the "Clear Completed" button to remove all completed tasks from your list.
