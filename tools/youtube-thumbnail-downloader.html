What are YouTube Thumbnails?

YouTube thumbnails are preview images that show on a video in YouTube. They are displayed on the YouTube website and App also and in search results. Thumbnails are used to give a preview of the video content and attract people to click on the video.

How to Download YouTube Thumbnails with this YouTube Thumbnail Downloader tool?

  1. Step 1: Enter the YouTube video URL in the given input field. Once you enter the URL, the tool will automatically fetch all sizes of thumbnails.
  2. Step 2: You can download the thumbnails in different sizes by clicking on the "Download" button below the thumbnail. Then right-click on the image and select "Save image as" to download the thumbnail.

FAQs

How to find the YouTube video URL?

To find the YouTube video URL, go to the YouTube website and open the video you want to download the thumbnail for. Copy the URL from the address bar of your browser. Or click on the "Share" button to get the URL. In the app, click on "Share" and then Copy the link.

Yes, this tool will fetch all sizes of thumbnails and you can download them easily.
