    Features

    • Auto Save: Your notes are saved locally in your browser, ensuring you never lose your work.

    Use Cases

    Privacy & Security

    The Online Notepad saves your notes locally in your browser's storage. This means that your notes are not sent to any server and are only accessible on the device you created them on. If you clear your browser's cache or switch devices, your notes will not be available. Always remember to download important notes if you want to keep them safe.
